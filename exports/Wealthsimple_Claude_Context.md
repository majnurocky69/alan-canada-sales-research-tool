Wealthsimple is a rapidly growing Toronto-based fintech company with an estimated annual revenue of $341.2M and total funding of $1.4B through its Series E round. Wealthsimple shows strong market penetration in Canada, managing over $100 billion in assets and serving more than 3 million clients.
