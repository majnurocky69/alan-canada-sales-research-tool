# Alan Canada Sales Context: Sidekick

**Target Propensity Score:** 70/100

## 1. Executive Summary & Growth Trajectory
Sidekick is a Toronto-based retail company specializing in muscle recovery and injury rehabilitation tools, founded by Hin Lai. With 15 employees and $330K in total funding at the 'Other' stage, the company recorded $1.1M in revenue in 2025 and has amassed over 250,000 customers. They operate with international shipping, demonstrating an expansion into new markets, and their product line, which includes HSA/FSA approved tools, is deeply aligned with health and wellness.

This signals a cultural compatibility with Alan Canada's digital-first platform, virtual care options, and efficient processes like 48-hour reimbursements. Emphasize how Alan can provide a seamless, modern health insurance experience that aligns with Sidekick's innovative approach to health and wellness.
